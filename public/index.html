Every day I hear strange sounds: a child and woman crying, gurgling, knocking, scratching on the door and, this morning, there was a knocking sound coming from behind the picture frame.

I am absolutely terrified. The most terrifying thing of all is that I do not feel the need to do the things that humans normally do: such as eat or sleep. Therefore I have been awake all this time: trapped in this hell and unable to break down the door to escape. All the windows are locked, and the glass cannot be shattered.
